About Rezz

Isabelle Rezazadeh is a Canadian DJ and record producer from Niagra Falls, Ontario. Originally born in Ukraine, she moved to Canada at a young age and began DJing at the age of 16. She released her debut EP, Insurrection back in 2015 through OWSLA's sub-label Nest HQ. Today she has thousands of fans all over the world with over 29k Spotify monthly listeners just in Los Angeles, CA alone. I had the pleasure of seeing her live both in Las Vegas, NV during a festival as well as in Los Angeles, CA for her Beyond the Senses Tour.
